A. User FAQs

How do I place an order?

To place an order:

  1. Search for restaurants or browse categories
  2. Select items and add them to your cart
  3. Review your order and proceed to checkout
  4. Select payment method and confirm order
  5. Track your order in real-time
How can I track my delivery?

Once your order is confirmed and a driver is assigned, you can track your delivery in real-time:

  • Go to "My Orders" in the app or website
  • Select the active order
  • View the live map with driver location
  • See estimated delivery time
How do I check my refund status?

To check your refund status:

  • Go to "Order History"
  • Select the order with the refund request
  • View refund status and details
  • Refunds typically process within 5-7 business days

If you have questions about your refund, contact our support team.

What should I do if my payment fails?

If your payment fails:

  • Check your payment method details
  • Ensure sufficient funds are available
  • Try a different payment method
  • Contact your bank if issues persist

If the problem continues, contact our support team for assistance.

How do I update my account information?

To update your account information:

  • Go to "Account Settings"
  • Select the information you want to update
  • Save your changes

For security reasons, some changes may require verification.

B. Restaurant FAQs

How do I receive orders from SmartEats?

Once you're onboarded as a SmartEats restaurant partner:

  • Orders will appear on your restaurant dashboard
  • You'll receive notifications for new orders
  • Accept orders within the specified time frame
  • Prepare orders for pickup by delivery drivers
How can I update my menu or prices?

To update your menu:

  • Log in to your restaurant dashboard
  • Navigate to the "Menu Management" section
  • Add, edit, or remove items as needed
  • Update prices and availability
  • Save your changes

Changes typically reflect on the platform within minutes.

What is the payout cycle for restaurants?

Our standard payout cycle is:

  • Weekly payouts: Processed every Monday
  • Processing time: 1-2 business days
  • Payment methods: Bank transfer or digital wallets

You can view your earnings and payout history in your restaurant dashboard.

Who should I contact for restaurant support?

For restaurant-specific support:

  • Email: restaurants@smarteats.com
  • Phone: +1 (555) 123-4567 (US) / +91 98765 43210 (India)
  • Dedicated support: Available through your restaurant dashboard

Our restaurant support team is available during business hours.

C. Driver FAQs

How do I accept delivery requests?

To accept delivery requests:

  • Ensure you're logged in and available in the driver app
  • Delivery requests will appear with order details
  • Accept requests within the specified time frame
  • Navigate to the restaurant for pickup
  • Confirm pickup and proceed to delivery location
What should I do if I have navigation issues?

If you experience navigation issues:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Ensure location services are enabled
  • Try alternative navigation apps if needed
  • Contact the customer for clarification if address is unclear
  • Report persistent issues to driver support
How and when will I receive my earnings?

Driver earnings are processed as follows:

  • Weekly payouts: Processed every Monday
  • Payment methods: Bank transfer or digital wallets
  • Earnings breakdown: Available in your driver dashboard
  • Instant cashout: Available for a small fee
What should I do if my account is suspended?

If your driver account is suspended:

  • Check your email for notification with details
  • Contact driver support for clarification
  • Provide any requested information or documentation
  • Follow the appeal process if you believe the suspension is in error

Common reasons for suspension include safety violations, low ratings, or policy breaches.

D. General FAQs

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team through:

  • Phone: +1 (555) 123-4567 (US) / +91 98765 43210 (India)
  • Email: support@smarteats.com
  • Live Chat: Available in the app and website during business hours
  • In-app Support: Use the help section in your account
What should I do in an emergency situation?

In case of emergency:

  • Medical emergency: Contact local emergency services immediately
  • Safety concern: Use the emergency button in the app if available
  • Accident: Ensure safety first, then contact support
  • Food allergy reaction: Seek medical attention immediately

After addressing the immediate emergency, contact our support team for assistance.

What should I do if my order is delayed?

If your order is delayed:

  • Check the order tracking for updates
  • Contact the restaurant if preparation is taking longer than expected
  • Contact support if the delay is significant
  • You may be eligible for compensation depending on the delay duration

Common reasons for delays include high demand, traffic, or weather conditions.

How can I provide feedback or file a complaint?

We welcome your feedback and take complaints seriously:

  • In-app feedback: Rate your order and provide comments
  • Email: feedback@smarteats.com
  • Complaints: Use the "Report Issue" feature in your order history
  • Social media: Message us on our official accounts

We aim to respond to all feedback within 24 hours.
