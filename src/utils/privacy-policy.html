Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last Updated:

A. General Information

SmartEats is a food delivery platform connecting Users (customers), Restaurants (partners), and Drivers (delivery partners).

Scope: This Privacy Policy applies to all Users, Restaurants, and Drivers using the SmartEats platform.

Definitions:

  • User: Individual ordering food through SmartEats
  • Restaurant: Food establishment partnering with SmartEats
  • Driver: Delivery partner providing delivery services
  • Personal Data: Any information relating to an identified or identifiable individual

B. Data Collection & Use

Personal Data Collected

  • Name, contact number, email address
  • Payment information (processed securely by payment partners)
  • Location data (for delivery optimization and tracking)
  • Device information and cookies
  • Restaurant business data (registration, menu, pricing)
  • Driver verification data (KYC documents, background checks)

Location Data Usage

We collect location data from Drivers for route optimization and delivery tracking, and from Users for accurate delivery location.

Account Management

SmartEats reserves the right to suspend accounts or implement "shadow banning" for violations of our terms, fraudulent activities, or to maintain platform safety.

C. How Data is Used

  • Order Processing: To facilitate food ordering and delivery
  • Fraud Detection: To identify and prevent fraudulent activities
  • Payments & Refunds: To process payments and manage refund requests
  • Deliveries & Tracking: To coordinate and track deliveries in real-time
  • Safety & Compliance: To ensure platform safety and regulatory compliance
  • Service Improvement: To enhance user experience and platform functionality

D. Data Sharing

We share your information only as necessary to provide our services:

  • With Restaurants: Only order details necessary for order fulfillment
  • With Drivers: Only delivery details necessary for order delivery
  • With Payment Processors: Payment data with Stripe, Razorpay, and other PCI-DSS compliant processors
  • With Government Authorities: Only when required by law or to protect our legal rights

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

E. Storage & Security

Data Protection

  • Industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest
  • PCI-DSS compliance for payment data handling
  • Regular security assessments and vulnerability testing

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law.

Compliance

We comply with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP) 2023 and incorporate GDPR-style user rights where applicable.

F. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Opt-out: Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@smarteats.com.

G. Children's Privacy

United States (COPPA): We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

Under 18 Safeguards: Users under 18 must have parent/guardian consent to use our services. We implement additional safeguards for minor users.

H. International Transfers

SmartEats operates in both the United States and India. Data may be transferred between these jurisdictions with appropriate safeguards in place to ensure protection equivalent to local data protection laws.

I. Disclaimer

SmartEats is not responsible for events outside our reasonable control, including but not limited to natural disasters, government actions, internet outages, or other force majeure events that may impact service delivery.
